About Us

The QALMS project is designed and developed catering the needs of the
average NUST student, who finds it quite annoying to check his grades
and attendance and other academic information, due to the way QALAM and
LMS are designed.

Our project simplifies everything and provides you
everything on one single page.

All you need is to login like you do on LMS and QALAM, and our web app will provide you with all the information without any struggle.
